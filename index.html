What do AHID TRADE Offer?

At AHID TRADE, we provide trusted brokerage and facilitation services in energy, minerals, and international trade. Our operations are designed for reliability, transparency, and global compliance.

Crude Oil (BLCO and other grades) Brokerage & Export

We connect vetted buyers with credible Nigerian BLCO and other grades sellers. Our services cover SPA structuring, documentation verification (POP, ATS, Q&Q), CIF/FOB delivery coordination, and full compliance with NNPC and international standards.

Refined Petroleum Products Trading

We trade AGO (Diesel), PMS (Petrol), Jet A1, and DPK to global buyers. We manage pricing, logistics, contracts (SPA, PB, DLC), and delivery to end users, refineries, and governments.

Mineral Import & Export

We broker, export & import gold, lithium, zinc, lead, tantalite, and columbite. We handle export licensing, verification, logistics, and documentation (NES, FIRS, MSDS) for international buyers.

Global Trade Facilitation

We connect international buyers and sellers, verify deals, structure contracts (SPA, IMFPA), and manage compliance. We reduce trade risk and support multi-party coordination globally.

Logistics & Documentation Support

We assist with trade paperwork commercial invoices, bill of lading, SGS, Q&Q and coordinate shipping, NOR/ETA, and POD delivery for smooth execution.

Contract Structuring & Advisory

We design safe, performance-driven trade contracts, advise on deal flow, and help new brokers or companies set up reliable trade structures, SPVs, and risk controls.
